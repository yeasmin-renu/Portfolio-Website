About Me.

Hello! I’m a Computer Science graduate (BSc) from American International University-Bangladesh(AIUB), Dhaka, Bangladesh, currently working as an Independent Researcher. I’m interested in working with classification and predictive analysis, pattern recognition, text classification and object detection. Till now, I have 4 publications in peer-reviewed journals and conference papers, where I have worked collaboratively with researches from Noakhali Science and Technology University, Brac University and American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) etc.

Currently, I’m working with different domains of data science and machine learning such as NLP, Image processing, GIS modeling and Machine learning.
